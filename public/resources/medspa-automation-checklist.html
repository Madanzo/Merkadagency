2. The 3 Critical Touchpoints to Automate Immediately

Touchpoint #1: Instant Lead Response (0-5 minutes)

What to automate: New inquiry acknowledgment

The Goal: Engage the lead while their intent is highest (within 5 minutes).

Automation Setup:

SMS Template:
Hi [Name]! Thanks for reaching out to [MedSpa Name]. We received your inquiry about [service]. Our treatment coordinator will call you within 30 minutes. - [Your Name]
Email Template:
Send detailed service information + booking link

Best Practice: Use an automated SMS "concierge" message immediately after a form fill. Email is too slow; phone calls often go to voicemail. SMS has a 98% open rate.

Tip: Don't just confirm receipt. Ask a question to start a conversation. E.g., "Thanks for inquiring about Botox! Are you looking for a morning or afternoon appointment?"

Tools needed: CRM with SMS/email automation

Touchpoint #2: Appointment Reminders (24h + 2h before)

What to automate: Appointment confirmations and reminders

The Goal: Reduce no-show rates to under 5%.

Automation Setup:

  • 24 hours before: Confirmation text with prep instructions
  • 2 hours before: Final reminder with parking/directions

Best Practice: Implement a "cadence of communication." Do not rely on a single reminder. A proven cadence is: confirmation immediately upon booking, reminder 48 hours prior (requires confirmation), and a final reminder 2 hours before.

Tools needed: Scheduling software with automated reminders

Touchpoint #3: Post-Treatment Follow-up (24h + 7 days + 30 days)

What to automate: Care instructions and review requests

The Goal: Increase retention and generate 5-star reviews.

Automation Setup:

  • 24 hours: "How are you feeling? Here's your aftercare guide..."
  • 7 days: Check-in + next appointment suggestion
  • 30 days: Review request + referral program introduction

Best Practice: Automate a "check-in" text 24 hours post-procedure. If they reply positively, trigger an automated review request link. If they reply with a concern, alert staff immediately.

3. Dead Lead Revival Strategy

Reviving "dead" leads is the lowest hanging fruit for revenue generation. These are prospects who inquired 3-6 months ago but never booked.

Strategy: The "9-Word Email" Approach

This approach works because it feels personal, not promotional. It requires no new staff, just a segmented list in your CRM.

Subject: Are you still looking?
Body: Hi [First Name], are you still looking to get [Treatment of Interest] done?

- [Your Name]

The 3-Email Revival Sequence

Email 1: The Personal Touch (Send immediately)
Subject: [Name], quick question about your [service] interest

Hi [Name],

I noticed you were interested in [service] a few weeks ago. I wanted to personally reach out because we just had a cancellation for this Friday at 2pm.

Would you like me to hold this spot for you? We also have 15% off for new clients this month.

Best,
[Your name]
Email 2: The Social Proof (Send 5 days later)
Subject: See [Name]'s amazing [service] results

Hi [Name],

I thought you'd love to see Sarah's transformation after just one [service] treatment.

[Before/after photos]

She said: "I wish I'd done this sooner! The results are better than I imagined."

We have openings next week if you're ready to start your journey.

[Book now button]
Email 3: The Last Call (Send 10 days later)
Subject: Final notice: Your consultation expires soon

Hi [Name],

This is my final email about the [service] consultation we discussed.

I'm removing you from our interest list tomorrow, but I wanted to give you one last chance to claim your complimentary consultation.

[Book consultation button]

If you're not interested, no worries - just ignore this email.

[Your name]

The SMS Revival Campaign

  • Day 1: "Hi [Name]! We miss you at [MedSpa]. New client special: 20% off any service this month. Book: [link]"
  • Day 14: "Last chance for 20% off! Schedule by Friday: [link]"
  • Day 30: "One final gift: Free skincare consultation + $50 credit. Valid this week only: [link]"

Timing & Frequency

  • Segment: Leads > 90 days old with no booking.
  • Channel: Send via SMS first, then Email if no response.
  • Cadence: One message. Wait 48 hours. If no reply, archive. Do not spam.
