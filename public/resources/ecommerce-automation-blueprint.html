2. Post-Purchase Automation

The sale is just the beginning. Here's how to turn one-time buyers into repeat customers:

The Post-Purchase Flow

  • Immediately: Order confirmation + what to expect
  • Shipped: Tracking info + estimated delivery
  • Delivered +1 Day: "How's everything?" check-in
  • Delivered +7 Days: Review request + referral offer
  • Delivered +30 Days: Replenishment reminder (if applicable)
  • Delivered +60 Days: "We miss you" + personalized recommendation
Key Metric: Aim for a 25%+ repeat purchase rate within 90 days.

3. Customer Segmentation

Not all customers are equal. Segment and treat them differently:

RFM Segmentation

  • Champions (Recent, Frequent, High Spend): VIP treatment, early access, loyalty rewards
  • Loyal Customers: Upsell, cross-sell, referral programs
  • At Risk: Win-back campaigns, special offers
  • Hibernating: Deep discount or let go
  • New Customers: Welcome series, education, social proof

5. VIP Program Structure

VIP customers drive 40-60% of revenue. Here's how to build a program that works:

Tier Structure

  • Bronze ($0-$199 LTV): Free shipping on $50+, birthday discount
  • Silver ($200-$499 LTV): 10% off all orders, early access to sales
  • Gold ($500+ LTV): 15% off, free shipping always, exclusive products, personal shopper

VIP Benefits That Work

  • Points for purchases (not just dollars ‚Äì engagement matters)
  • Referral bonuses (give $20, get $20)
